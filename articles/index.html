RING ROTATION

weekly entries on which rings I choose to wear on certain days and why.

i've been collecting rings for a couple months now. whether i'm on vacation or just checking out a new thrift store, i find myself accumulating new rings quite often. i'm most attracted to chunky, odd rings; anything that grabs my attention.

every week i will document my "ring rotation" - aka which rings i chose to wear for that week. i try to pick my ring rotation with purpose and intent. perhaps to match with my outfit or to correlate with somewhere i'm going.
